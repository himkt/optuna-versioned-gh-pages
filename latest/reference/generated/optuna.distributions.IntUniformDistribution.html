optuna.distributions.IntUniformDistribution¶

class optuna.distributions.IntUniformDistribution(low, high, step=1)[source]¶

A uniform distribution on integers.

This object is instantiated by suggest_int(), and passed to samplers in general.

Note

If the range \([\mathsf{low}, \mathsf{high}]\) is not divisible by \(\mathsf{step}\), \(\mathsf{high}\) will be replaced with the maximum of \(k \times \mathsf{step} + \mathsf{low} < \mathsf{high}\), where \(k\) is an integer.

low¶

Lower endpoint of the range of the distribution. low is included in the range.

high¶

Upper endpoint of the range of the distribution. high is included in the range.

step¶

A step for spacing between values.

Raises

ValueError – If low value is larger than high value, or step value is smaller or equal to 0.
